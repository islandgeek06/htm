Victoria Romer

Was born in Sylmar, California and at just four years old Victoria relocated to Texas with her mother and three siblings, an older sister and two younger siblings. Having to deal with the tragic turnings of being removed from her mother. Victoria was left to deal with the harsh doings and reality of experiencing the foster system as a youth with her siblings. After spending four years within the foster system, it was not until Victoria and her siblings were given another chance at love and life. The experience being fostered by Victoria and siblings being adopted by the Romero family. Victoria Romero grew up in a loving family of eight. While becoming into her own as individual as she attended La Porte High School. She furthered her secondary academia affairs at San Jacinto College as well Houston Community College (HCC).

Having given birth to several entrepreneurial endeavors is what led Victoria to hit the ground running with the upmost confidence in believing she was ready to take on the world. Victoria has always had a passion in wanting to assist others to become greater for themselves. Ms. Romero is the mother of two, consultant and is also a brand ambassador for several companies in which she is still active with today.
